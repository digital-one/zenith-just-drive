How it works

Drive a brand new, fully insured, taxed car
by giving up a small part of your gross salary each month.
You save money by not paying Income Tax or National Insurance
on the amount you ‘sacrifice’.

The monthly amount you 'sacrifice' also
includes breakdown cover,
replacement tyres, windscreen and glass cover.
Everything except fuel.
