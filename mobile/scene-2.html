Just Drive

How it works

Drive a brand new, fully insured, taxed car by giving up a small part of your gross salary each month. You save money by not paying Income Tax or National Insurance on the amount you 'sacrifice'.

The monthly amount you 'sacrifice' also includes:

• Breakdown cover
• Replacement tyres
• Windscreen and glass cover

Everything except fuel.
